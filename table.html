Month

12 months combine to make a complete year, some months have longer days than others.

february have 28 or 29 days, April, June, September and November have 30 days each.

the remaining 7 months have 31 days each like the exmple above.

Weeks

4 weeks combine to make a complete Month.

each week have 7 days from Monday to Sunday.

altogether, we have 56 weeks in a year.

Days

in a week we have 5 working days and 2 weekends.

From Monday to Friday we work, and Sunday and Saturday we relax to work on other things.
